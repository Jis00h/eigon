— Services

Two facets.
One rigorous practice.

Data Analysis

End-to-end analysis of complex biological datasets. RNA-seq, single-cell, WGS/WES, metagenomics, proteomics, clinical data. Deliverables include documented code, publication-ready figures, and a written interpretation report.

Pipeline Development

Custom bioinformatics pipelines built for your lab's specific data types and workflows. Reproducible, scalable, and documented. Built in Snakemake or Nextflow, with R and Python. Designed to be handed over and maintained by your team.

— About

One researcher. One point of contact. Full accountability.

Eigon Research is an independent bioinformatics practice led by Jisoo Han. Jisoo holds a Master's and Bachelor's degree from South Korea and works exclusively with research labs and scientific teams on complex data analysis and computational workflow development. Every engagement is handled directly — no junior analysts, no outsourcing. The name Eigon is drawn from eigenvectors — mathematical structures that reveal the underlying geometry of complex data. It reflects a commitment to finding signal in complexity.

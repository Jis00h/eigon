Data Analysis

For researchers, labs, and biotech teams

End-to-end analytical work on complex biological datasets. I handle the full pipeline — from raw data QC through to statistical analysis, visualization, and written interpretation — and deliver documented code, publication-ready figures, and a comprehensive analysis report.

Capabilities
  • RNA-seq differential expression analysis
  • Whole genome / exome variant calling and annotation
  • 16S rRNA and metagenomic profiling
  • Single-cell RNA-seq analysis (Seurat, Scanpy)
  • Proteomics data processing and analysis
  • Pathway and gene ontology enrichment
  • Survival and clinical data analysis
  • Custom pipeline development (R, Python, Snakemake, Nextflow)
  • Reproducible, documented workflows

— About

One researcher.
Full accountability.

Eigon Research is an independent bioinformatics practice led by Jisoo Han. Jisoo holds a Master's and Bachelor's degree from South Korea and specializes in the analysis of complex biological and genomic datasets. Every project is handled directly — not outsourced, not passed to junior analysts. When you work with Eigon Research, you know exactly who is responsible for the work.

The name Eigon is drawn from eigenvectors — mathematical objects that reveal the underlying structure in complex data. It reflects the practice's core commitment: finding signal in complexity, and communicating it with precision.

Jisoo also works with undergraduate and master's students navigating their first serious research projects. Having completed rigorous academic training in South Korea, she understands what strong research looks like at every stage — and how to help students get there.
